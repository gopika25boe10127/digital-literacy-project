# Stay Safe Online Checklist

This document provides a guide for students to recognize digital threats. Following these actionable steps ensures a safer experience while navigating academic and financial platforms.

---

## Core Safety Recommendations

* **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable 2FA on all email and social media accounts to add an extra layer of security.
* **Password Management:** Use a unique, complex password for every platform and update them quarterly.
* **UPI Safety (Receiving Money):** Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. A PIN is only required for sending or checking balances.
* **UPI Safety (Verification):** Always verify the merchant or recipient name displayed on the app before authorizing any transaction.
* **Link Vigilance:** Avoid clicking on shortened URLs or unsolicited links sent via SMS or WhatsApp.
* **Secure Networking:** Refrain from using public Wi-Fi for banking or accessing sensitive academic portals.
* **App Permissions:** Regularly review app permissions and revoke access to your camera, contacts, or location for unnecessary apps.
* **System Maintenance:** Keep your device operating system and antivirus software updated to patch security vulnerabilities.

---

## Reporting Channels

If you are a victim of cybercrime, immediately contact:

* **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
* **National Helpline Number:** 1930
