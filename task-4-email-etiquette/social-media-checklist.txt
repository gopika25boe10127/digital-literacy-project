Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist
social-media-checklist
The Do’s
Do set your profiles to private and regularly audit your follower lists.
Do use a professional headshot and a clean bio that reflects your academic interests.
Do share your project milestones, certifications, and university achievements to build a positive digital shadow.
Do verify the credibility of news or information before hitting the share button.
Do use two-factor authentication (2FA) on all platforms to prevent unauthorized access.
The Don’ts
Don’t post your real-time location, home address, or sensitive documents like your Student ID.
Don’t engage in "flame wars" or heated public arguments that could damage your reputation.
Don’t post content depicting illegal activities or violations of university policy.
Don’t vent about professors, internship coordinators, or peers on public feeds.
Don’t click on suspicious links in DMs, even if they appear to come from friends.
